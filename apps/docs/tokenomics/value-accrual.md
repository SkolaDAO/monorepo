## Value Drivers

### 1. Staking Demand (Supply Lock)

More creators = more tokens locked in stakes.

If 10,000 creators stake $50 average:
- $500,000 worth of $LEARN locked
- Reduced circulating supply
- Price support

### 2. Payment Demand (Buy Pressure)

Learners buy $LEARN for 5% discount.

If $1M monthly GMV:
- Significant buy pressure
- Continuous demand

### 3. Buyback & Burn (Supply Reduction)

20% of protocol fees buy and burn $LEARN.

- Permanent supply reduction
- Deflationary over time
- Value accrues to all holders

### 4. Fee Distribution (Yield)

40% of fees to stakers = real yield.

Not inflationary rewards. Real revenue from real sales.

## Why Wait to Launch?

Unlike "learn-to-earn" tokens that print rewards from day 1:

- **No inflation** - Fixed supply
- **Real revenue** - Fees from actual sales first
- **Proven model** - We know the economics work

We're building real usage before adding a token. The token should capture existing value, not create artificial hype.
