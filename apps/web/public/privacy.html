Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Skola ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Skola platform at app.skola.academy and related mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our decentralized course marketplace.

Key Points:
  • We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services
  • Your wallet address is your identity — we don't require email or personal info
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • Course progress and purchases are stored to provide the service

1. Information We Collect

Wallet Information: When you connect your cryptocurrency wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Rainbow, WalletConnect), we collect your public wallet address. This is used to identify your account, process purchases, and deliver course access.

Profile Information (Optional): You may optionally provide a username and profile avatar. This information is publicly visible on courses you create or reviews you leave.

Course Data: We store information about courses you create, purchase, or enroll in, including:

  • Course progress and completion status
  • Purchase history and transaction records
  • Reviews and ratings you submit
  • Messages sent through course chat or direct messages

Technical Data: We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our platform:

  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Referral source

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process course purchases and creator payouts
  • Track your course progress and completion
  • Enable communication between creators and students
  • Send notifications about your courses and purchases
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Blockchain Transactions

Skola is built on the Base blockchain (Ethereum Layer 2). When you make a purchase or receive payment:

  • Transactions are processed on-chain and are publicly visible
  • Transaction records are immutable and cannot be deleted
  • Your wallet address and transaction amounts are publicly accessible on the blockchain

We do not control blockchain data. This is inherent to how decentralized systems work.

4. Video Hosting

Course videos are hosted on Bunny Stream, a third-party video hosting service. Videos are protected with signed URLs and cannot be downloaded. Bunny Stream may collect technical data as described in their privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate our platform (hosting, video delivery, analytics)
  • Course Creators: Creators can see the wallet addresses of students enrolled in their courses
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Course purchase records and blockchain transactions are permanent. You may request deletion of optional profile information at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (where technically feasible)
  • Object to certain processing of your data
  • Data portability

Note: Some data (like blockchain transactions) cannot be modified or deleted due to the nature of decentralized systems.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure video delivery with signed URLs
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data

9. Children's Privacy

Skola is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. International Users

Skola operates globally. By using our platform, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
